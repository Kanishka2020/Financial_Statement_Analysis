# Financial_Statement_Analysis
A financial statement is a formal record of the financial activities and position of a business, organization, or individual. It shows how money is earned, spent, and managed over a period of time.

In this project I have analyse the Finanial Statement.

Analyzing financial statements helps in understanding the true health and performance of a business. Here are the key reasons:

 ### 1. Assess Profitability
    Determines whether the company is earning enough profit.
    Helps track trends in income and expenses.
### 2. Evaluate Financial Position
     Shows whether the company has enough assets to cover its liabilities.
     Helps understand stability and solvency.
### 3. Measure Liquidity
     Indicates the ability to meet short-term obligations.
     Important for avoiding cash shortages.

**Definition**: Income Statement let us know that what **Profit** we get after removing **Expenses** from **Total Sales**.

**Profit** are in three category:

- **EBIDTA**: (Operating Profit in Cash terms): EBIDTA gives Profit value receives after removing Operating expenses.

- **EBIT**: Profit after removing Depriciation & Amortization

- **Profit of the year**: Profit after removing Interest & Taxes
